Welcome to let's care for Earth

Climate change and environment protection has always been a hot topic for humanity but little to no actions has been taken regarding this issue.

The dried forest image above is a concrete proof of our alarming situation

According to NOAA's 2021 Annual Climate Report the combined land and ocean temperature has increased at an average rate of 0.14 degrees Fahrenheit (0.08 degrees Celsius) per decade since 1880; however, the average rate of increase since 1981 has been more than twice as fast: 0.32 °F (0.18 °C) per decade.

The increase of greenhouse gas emission is the main reason behind this rise in temperature.

We should start taking actions to protect our dear Mother Earth because we only have one earth and we need to preserve it for future generations. Together, we can achieve great things.
